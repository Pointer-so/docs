## Overview

Pointer's integration system allows you to connect various third-party services to enhance the contextual understanding and functionality of your guides.&#x20;

By connecting these integrations, Pointer can provide more accurate, contextual walkthroughs and responses by leveraging data from your connected services.

You can add integrations on the [Knowledge](https://app.pointer.so/knowledge) page of our dashboard.
