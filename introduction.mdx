## What is Pointer?

[Pointer](https://pointer.so) is a platform that guides users through software products in real-time.&#x20;

When users get stuck, Pointer shows them exactly what to do (in real-time!). No more support tickets, doc searches, or annoyed emails.

To get started, check out our [Quickstart](https://docs.pointer.so/quickstart) guide.
